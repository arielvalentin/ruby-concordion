assertEquals

Here is an example of a basic assertion made with assertEquals.

The greeting should be <b concordion:assertEquals="getGreeting()">Hello World!</b>.

The relevent fragments of the tag are:

  • concordion - a namespace that browsers will not attempt to interpret.
  • assertEquals - the concordion command to invoke.
  • getGreeting - the fixture method to invoke, delegating to the system under test.
  • Hello World! - the expected output from the fixture.
