#TEXT

Here is an example of how to use the implicit concordion #TEXT variable.

When joining <quote concordion:execute="#result = concat(#TEXT,#TEXT)">Goodspeed</quote> with itself
the result should be: <quote concordion:assertEquals="#result">Goodspeed:Goodspeed</quote>.

The relevent fragments of the tag are:

  • #TEXT - bound to the contents out the tag, in this case 'Goodspeed'
  • concat - the fixture method being given 'Goodspeed', 'Goodspeed' as arguments.
  • #result - the concordion variable to store the result in.
