assertTrue

Here is an example of how to use the assertTrue command.

The boiling point of water (in Farenheit) is <p concordion:assertTrue="is_boiling_point(#TEXT)">212</p>.

The relevent fragments of the tag are:

  • #TEXT - bound to the contents out the tag, in this case 'Goodspeed'

And here's what it looks like:

The boiling point of water (in Farenheit) is 212.

And here's what a failure looks like:

The boiling point of water (in Farenheit) is211.
