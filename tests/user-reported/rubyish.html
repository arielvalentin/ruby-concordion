Rubyish Annotations

Here is an example of some of the ways Ruby-Concordion is rubyish.

<span concordion:execute="#result = concat #TEXT, #TEXT">AA</span>
<span concordion:assertEquals="#result">AA:AA</span>
<span concordion:assertEquals="no_args">foo</span>
<span concordion:assertEquals="return_arg #TEXT">foo</span>

The relevent fragments of the tag are:

  • concat #TEXT, #TEXT - argument lists without parentheses.
  • no_args - direct method invocations, equivalent to 'no_args()'

And here's what it looks like:

AA AA:AA foo foo
